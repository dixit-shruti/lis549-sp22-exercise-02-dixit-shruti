Description

Downtown San Diego is located at the south of San Diego. It has a rich restaurant scene and is well known for its "Little Italy" area with Italian restaurants.

How to get there?

From the airport

  1. Drive South towards Sasafras street and take a left turn.
  2. Drive for 1 mile and then take a right on 5th Avenue. After 0.5 miles, you will arrive at downtown.

By bus

  1. Walk South towards Sasafras street and continue walking for 0.2 miles to bus stop # 150.
  2. Take route 53 and ride 5 stops to arrive at downtown.
